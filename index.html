About the class

How do organisms evolve to deal with adversity? How does a cell control which genes are being expressed and when? How does a developing animal know its head from its tail? These are some of the big questions we address in Bi 1x. We explore the great ideas of biology not just with our minds, but with our hands, with microscopes, and with computers.

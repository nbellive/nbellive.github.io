    • Biology by the numbers

  • Bionumbers. This website is a database of useful biological numbers (with references!). It will help you find both key numbers characterizing biological systems as well as the experimental sources for those numbers. This complements the approach we will often take of trying to determine those numbers by thinking them up.
  • BioTheNumbers. Another website of useful biological numbers in ecosystems and how human influence has changed them.
  • E. coli statistics. This website gives some of the vital statistics for E. coli.

Lab Schedule

The class meets every Monday (except Memorial Day and June 2) from 7-10pm and every Tuesday from 9am-12pm. We will also have Thursday night (7-10pm) meetings from time to time. We will not meet in the event that Ditch Day falls on one of the scheduled class days, but we will schedule a make-up.

Below is a schedule of experiments, journal clubs, tutorials, and due dates. Prior to each lab, with the exception of week 1 and sea urchin fertilization, a prelab is due at the beginning of the lab period. All information for the prelabs, protocols, tutorials, and journal clubs can be found by clicking on the links below. This schedule will be closely followed, but is subject to change.

TA office hours are the only scheduled activities where attendance is not required. Unless otherwise specified, they are on Thursday nights at 7pm in the lab conference room. Instructor office hours are by appointment.
